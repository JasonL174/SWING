Problem

The problem that we are looking to create a robotic solution for is navigating a camera (or other data-collecting devices) through trees in a forest or jungle. However, given the length of time and size of the group, a functioning prototype for this is well out of the scope of the class. Because of this, our focus will be on solving the problem of moving a camera along an array of monkey bars - an initial step towards surveilling a jungle environment. We propose a brachiating robot which will swing between and along the bars from one hold to another, similar to how some monkeys move.
